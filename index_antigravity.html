The Offer

An AI workflow audit for the tasks that are too costly for staffing but too small for custom software.

For most small businesses and practices, there is a gap in operational efficiency. The tasks are too small and numerous to assign to a full-time employee, but too important to ignore. They are the revenue-critical tasks that fall through the cracks due to time constraints and competing priorities.
